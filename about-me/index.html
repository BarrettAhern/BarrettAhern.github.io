About Me

eCommerce consultant. 15+ years. Based in London.

Hi, I'm Barrett 👋

I'm an eCommerce consultant with 15+ years in the game, currently based in London and helping brands grow smarter and faster online.

I work at Vervaunt, a leading eCommerce consultancy and one of a handful of Shopify Plus Platinum Partners worldwide. I focus on enterprise replatforming, international expansion, and eCommerce strategy for some of the most interesting names in fashion, beauty, and lifestyle.

Before that, I was a Senior Product Manager at WeMakeWebsites (recognized by Shopify's Harley Finkelstein as "one of the most successful ecommerce agencies on the planet") and BORN Group, where I helped high-end brands build beautiful, high-performing online stores.

I marry business goals with tech reality. Whether that means leading a complex replatform to Shopify Plus, building a roadmap for international growth, or fine-tuning a tech stack, I'm all about practical strategies that drive real-world results.

A few things I'm good at:

  • Replatforming at scale — especially to Shopify Plus
  • Luxury fashion & beauty eCommerce — tailored for brand-led growth
  • Internationalisation & localisation — let's go global
  • Tech stack strategy & optimisation — clean, scalable setups
  • Conversion, performance & SEO — I play nice with both data and devs

Originally from Chicago — I've lived in Seattle, Luxembourg, and now London. Yes, I absolutely have food and drink recs for each. Want a Belgian-style Saison in Chicago, Seattle's best pizza, or the best neighbourhood pub in North London? I've got you.

I'm also a dad, a lover of slow food, a fan of well-made things (from watches to workwear), and a sucker for a good map.

If you want to chat about eCommerce or debate the best burger in North London, get in touch.
