About me

Barrett Ahern

I’m an eCommerce consultant based in London, working with enterprise fashion, beauty, and lifestyle brands on platform strategy, international expansion, and commercial performance. I specialise in Shopify Plus, having led some of the most complex migrations and builds in the industry.

I’ve spent fifteen years at the intersection of technical execution and commercial strategy — working agency side at WeMakeWebsites and BORN Group, and in-house for brands like K2 Sports and Just Kids Store. That breadth gives me an unusual vantage point: I understand how great eCommerce is built, what gets in the way of it, and how to align teams around the decisions that matter.
