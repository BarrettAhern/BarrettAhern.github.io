Background

Barrett Ahern — eCommerce Strategist

Lead eCommerce Strategist at Vervaunt. Shopify Plus architecture, enterprise replatforming, and international growth. 15+ years. Based in London.

I'm an eCommerce strategist based in London with 15 years across platform architecture, international expansion, and commercial strategy. Currently a consultant at Vervaunt, a London-based eCommerce consultancy and Shopify Plus Platinum Partner, where I work on enterprise replatforming, tech stack strategy, and international growth for fashion, beauty, and lifestyle brands.

My work sits at the intersection of technical architecture and commercial performance. I'm as comfortable evaluating data schema and metadata structure as I am conducting TCO analysis or translating KPI frameworks into C-suite reporting.
