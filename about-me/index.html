About Me

eCommerce strategy and platform architecture. 15+ years. Based in London.

I'm an eCommerce strategist based in London, currently consulting at Vervaunt on enterprise replatforming and eCommerce strategy for fashion, beauty, and lifestyle brands. Fifteen years in, across platform architecture, international expansion, and the commercial decisions that connect them.

My background starts in-house. From 2011 to 2015, I was Head of Ecommerce at Just Kids Store in Chicago, owning the end-to-end customer journey, technical roadmap, and commercial strategy. The centrepiece was a replatform from a bespoke legacy system onto Magento, which delivered a 180% conversion uplift and grew organic traffic by over 1,200%. From there I moved to K2 Sports in Seattle as eCommerce Delivery Manager (2016 to 2018), running the digital product roadmap across a portfolio of ten-plus heritage sports brands, including K2 Skis, Full Tilt, and Line Skis, translating brand-specific requirements into a unified technical backlog and managing the multidisciplinary team delivering it. Working brand-side at that scale meant understanding trading performance as clearly as technical delivery: what the exec team needed to see, what the numbers were actually saying, and what it cost to run the channel. That perspective shapes how I approach every engagement.

From there, I spent four years as Senior Product Owner at WeMakeWebsites (recognised by Shopify's Harley Finkelstein as one of the most successful ecommerce agencies on the planet), leading replatforming programmes and managing cross-functional delivery teams for fashion and lifestyle brands. Before joining Vervaunt, I was Senior Product Manager at BORN Group, focused on enterprise eCommerce strategy and platform architecture for luxury clients.

The work I do now tends to sit at the junction of commercial and technical: replatforming at scale, internationalisation, tech stack selection, and the measurement frameworks that underpin serious trading. I'm equally at home in a conversation about revenue and growth reporting with a leadership team and in the detail of a data architecture or integration spec.

Originally from Chicago, I've lived in Seattle, Luxembourg, and now London. Yes, I absolutely have food and drink recs for each. Want a Belgian-style Saison in Chicago, Seattle's best pizza, or the best neighbourhood pub in North London? I've got you.
