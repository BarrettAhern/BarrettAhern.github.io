What I do

Services

eCommerce Strategy

Navigating the complexities of global commerce requires a leader who aligns technical architecture with business profitability. I specialise in developing long-term digital roadmaps for luxury and premium DTC brands, ensuring that every technology investment is a strategic driver for revenue, customer loyalty, and brand equity.

Replatforming & Migrations

I lead the end-to-end lifecycle of enterprise-level migrations, acting as the bridge between technical integrity and commercial goals. My focus is on managing complex change management, ensuring a seamless transition that serves as a high-velocity engine for revenue without compromising brand equity during "step-change" moments.

International Expansion

Scaling into international markets requires a balance of technical confidence and cultural fluency. I architect bespoke global strategies that navigate the intricacies of cross-border logistics, localised CX, and Shopify Markets. My approach ensures brands scale rapidly while maintaining operational consistency and a unified global identity.

CX & Revenue Optimisation

I drive revenue growth through a full-funnel approach to Customer Experience and experimentation. From UX and journey redesign (PLP, PDP, Checkout) to AOV growth and personalisation, I implement rigorous experimentation frameworks that deliver measurable uplift in conversion rates and long-term customer lifetime value.

Tech Stack Optimisation

I view technology selection as a primary lever for commercial acceleration. By leading discovery-led platform selection and TCO (Total Cost of Ownership) modelling, I de-risk high-stakes CAPEX investments. I ensure the chosen stack provides the agility and scalability to drive enterprise value for brands scaling toward IPO or global dominance.

Analytics & Data Strategy

Operational maturity is built on data integrity. I define measurement frameworks and KPI ownership, overseeing omnichannel data architecture and GA4 strategy. By establishing clear trading cadences and data-driven governance, I provide the visibility necessary for high-level P&L management and informed commercial decision-making.
